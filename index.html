Meet The Director

Jed's Been Building This Since He Was 13.

Jed's fondest childhood memories were summer camp. By age 13 his goal was clear: own and operate a world-class bass fishing camp for kids who loved to fish more than anything else.

Since then, he's built the credentials to back it up. He started as a CIT at his local camp in New Jersey, went on to work with the Boys & Girls Clubs in Gainesville, FL, served as director of tennis at Candlewood Isle, and spent 16 years teaching middle school.

At Candlewood Fishing Camp, your young angler will connect with nature, make lifelong friends, and catch some of the biggest bass of their life.
